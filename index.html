About FlipIt

FlipIt is a decentralized application that lets you toss a coin in a trustless and secure environment. Powered by blockchain, your coin flips are recorded on the ledger for full transparency. Whether you're here for fun or strategy, FlipIt is your coin toss destination.

How to Play

To play FlipIt, simply click on the 'Play Now' button. Once you're in the game, you can choose between heads or tails. Make your prediction, toss the coin, and see if you win! The coin flip is powered by smart contracts, ensuring fairness and transparency.

No matter if you win or lose, the experience is the same. Every flip is secure, recorded on the blockchain, and provides an exciting way to engage with decentralized technology.
